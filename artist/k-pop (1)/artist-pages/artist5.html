Stray Kids

K-pop 2017 – Present

About Artist

Stray Kids debuted in 2017 under JYP Entertainment, known for self-produced music and bold concepts. Members—Bang Chan, Lee Know, Changbin, Hyunjin, Han, Felix, Seungmin, and I.N—write lyrics and compose. They focus on youth struggles, empowerment, and self-confidence. The group has rapidly gained international recognition. They participate in tours, fan meetings, and charity work. Stray Kids balances creative control with commercial success. Their journey reflects determination, authenticity, and innovation. They continue evolving as artists and performers.

Career Achievements
