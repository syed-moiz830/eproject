BTS (방탄소년단)

K-pop 2012 – Present

About Artist

BTS formed in 2013 under Big Hit Entertainment in Seoul. Seven members—RM, Jin, Suga, J-Hope, Jimin, V, and Jungkook—blend vocals, rap, and choreography. They broke barriers for K-pop globally, performing at the Grammys and U.S. arenas. BTS’s music often explores youth, mental health, and self-love. Each member also has solo projects showcasing individual styles. They are philanthropists through UNICEF campaigns. Their fandom, ARMY, is among the most dedicated worldwide. BTS continues to shape K-pop while influencing global pop culture.
