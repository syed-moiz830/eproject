Wolfgang Amadeus Mozart

classical-period 1756 – Present

About Artist

Mozart was an Austrian prodigy and one of the most prolific and influential composers of the Classical Period. He began composing at age five and toured Europe as a child virtuoso. Known for his operas, symphonies, concertos, and chamber music, Mozart’s compositions are admired for their clarity, balance, and emotional depth. His music embodies elegance, wit, and dramatic expressiveness, and he was a central figure in Vienna’s musical life until his untimely death at 35..
