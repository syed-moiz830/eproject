GoGo Penguin

nu-jazz 2012 – Present

About Artist

GoGo Penguin fuses jazz with electronic minimalism. Their music is instrumental and cinematic. They use repetitive rhythms and textures. The trio format feels expansive. They draw inspiration from ambient music. Their sound is modern yet emotional. They appeal to electronic listeners. They redefine acoustic jazz.
