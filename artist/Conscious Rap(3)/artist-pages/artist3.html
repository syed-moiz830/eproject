Dave

Conscious-rap 2015 – Present

About Artist

Dave grew up in South London facing family trauma. Music became his emotional outlet. He gained recognition through social media freestyles. His lyrics focused on pain, race, and identity. Psychodrama explored therapy and inner conflict. The album showed maturity beyond his age. Dave avoided hype and focused on substance. He is now one of the UK’s smartest lyricists.
