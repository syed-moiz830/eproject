Lorenzo Bellavere

Historic-classic 1988 – Present

About Artist

Bellavere is a composer-scholar devoted to Baroque structure and counterpoint. Educated in Italy, he studies Bach and Palestrina while composing modern works inspired by strict musical discipline. His music emphasizes balance, ornamentation, and intellectual elegance.

Career Achievements

1990

Published academic papers on Baroque harmony

1991

Directed early-music ensembles across Europe

1994

Revived contrapuntal composition in modern classical circles
