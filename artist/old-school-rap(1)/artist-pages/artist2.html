Wu-Tang Clan

Old-school-rap 1992 – Present

About Artist

Wu-Tang Clan formed in Staten Island, New York. Members bonded over poverty and kung-fu films. RZA crafted a dark, minimalist sound. They rejected flashy commercial rap. 36 Chambers introduced a new lyrical aggression. Each member developed a solo career. Wu-Tang became a global brand. They remain one of rap’s most influential groups.
