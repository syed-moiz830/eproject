Bohemia

Desi-rap 2002 – Present

About Artist

Bohemia was born in Karachi and raised in California. He faced homelessness after losing his parents early. Music became his survival and identity. He created Punjabi rap when no scene existed. Pesa Nasha Pyar showed harsh truths of life. His sound influenced generations of rappers. Despite controversies, his legacy stayed strong. He is widely regarded as desi hip-hop’s pioneer.
