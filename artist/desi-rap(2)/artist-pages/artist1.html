Talhah Yunus

Desi-rap 2012 – Present

About Artist

Talhah Yunus was born and raised in Karachi and developed a love for poetry at a young age. He started rapping in his teens and formed the duo Young Stunners with Talha Anjum. Early struggles and criticism shaped his emotionally intense writing style. Yunus became known for introspective, melancholic, and thoughtful lyrics. My Terrible Mind reflected his internal battles and personal growth. He prefers artistic integrity over mainstream fame. His work often touches themes of pain, ambition, and self-reflection. Today, he stands as one of Pakistan’s most lyrical hip-hop voices.
