Layne Staley

Grunge Rock 1986 – Present

About Artist

Layne Staley was born in 1967 in Washington. He joined Alice in Chains and brought a haunting vocal style. The band mixed grunge with heavy metal. Staley’s lyrics reflected addiction and inner suffering. Despite success, he avoided public attention. Drug addiction heavily affected his life and career. The band reduced activity due to his health. Layne Staley died in 2002, remembered for his raw honesty.
