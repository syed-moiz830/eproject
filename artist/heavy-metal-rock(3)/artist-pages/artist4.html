Judas Priest

Heavy-Metal 1969 – Present

About Artist

Judas Priest emerged from Birmingham’s heavy music scene. They replaced blues influences with a pure metal sound. The band introduced leather and studs as metal fashion. Their albums evolved with changing metal trends. Rob Halford became an iconic metal frontman. Internal tensions led to brief separations. Their reunion reignited fan enthusiasm worldwide. They continue releasing music decades later.
