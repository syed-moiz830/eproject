Dance-pop

Dance‑pop is a popular genre of pop music that blends catchy melodic hooks with upbeat rhythms designed for dancing and club play. It takes the accessibility of mainstream pop — simple choruses, memorable verses, and polished production — and adds electronic beats, driving bass lines, and bright synth textures that make you move. Dance‑pop tracks often draw from electronic dance music (EDM), disco, and house influences, balancing radio‑friendly song structures with energetic grooves. Artists in this genre aim to create songs that work both on the charts and on the dance floor.
