Hard Rock

Hard rock is characterized by aggressive guitar riffs, strong bass lines, and powerful drumming. It often features energetic and emotive vocals that convey passion and intensity. The genre influenced many later styles, including heavy metal and alternative rock. Hard rock bands are known for their electrifying live performances and stage presence. Lyrics often explore themes like rebellion, love, and personal struggles. It remains popular worldwide, appealing to fans who enjoy raw energy and powerful sound.
