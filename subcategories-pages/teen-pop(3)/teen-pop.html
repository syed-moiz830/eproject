Teen-pop

Teen pop is a subgenre of pop music that’s created, marketed, and oriented primarily toward teenagers and preteens. It typically features catchy melodies, danceable beats, and relatable lyrics about themes like love, friendship, heartbreak, growing up, and self‑discovery — topics that resonate strongly with teen experiences. The artists themselves are often young (sometimes teens when they debut), which helps fans connect personally and socially with their music.
